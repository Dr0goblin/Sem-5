Top 3 Trekking Destinations in Nepal

Nepal is home to some of the world's most spectacular trekking routes. The three destinations shown above are among the most popular:

  • Everest Base Camp Trek: The most famous trek in the world, offering views of Mount Everest and other Himalayan giants.
  • Annapurna Circuit Trek: A diverse trek through varied landscapes, from subtropical forests to high alpine terrain.
  • Langtang Valley Trek: Known as the "valley of glaciers," this trek offers beautiful scenery and rich cultural experiences.
