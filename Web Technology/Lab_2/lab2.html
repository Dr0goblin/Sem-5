Sports Week

ACHS College is excited to announce its annual Sports Week, a vibrant celebration of athletic spirit and teamwork. This event brings together students from all departments to participate in various sports activities, fostering camaraderie and promoting a healthy lifestyle. From thrilling football matches to intense basketball games, the week promises excitement and unforgettable moments.

The Sports Week also includes fun events like table-tennis, chess, and cultural performances, ensuring there's something for everyone. It's not just about competition; it's about building lasting friendships and memories. Join us in cheering for your teams and making this Sports Week one of the best yet!
